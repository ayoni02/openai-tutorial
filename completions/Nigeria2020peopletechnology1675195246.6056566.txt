In 2020, Nigeria has reached artificial general intelligence (AGI). This has caused a major shift in the way people live and work. The technology is now able to think and learn on its own, making many jobs obsolete. This has caused a lot of unemployment, as well as a decrease in productivity in many industries. The government has had to enact policies to help people adjust to this new reality. Some people have welcomed the change, while others have been resentful. Overall, the change has been difficult for many people, but the country has adapting and continues to thrive.
